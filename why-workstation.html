Workstation brings productive people together through shared workspaces, special events, social spaces and our digital member network. We were made by this community and we are here to serve its members. Love your work and work where you love.

Our Core Values

PIONEERS

We breed creators through our functional, innovative and flexible space empowered by cutting edge technology available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

CREATIVITY

We foster the exchange of ideas and solutions amongst our community. Create. Connect. Collaborate.

DEVELOPMENT

We’re a support system and knowledge center: from a directory of resources to promotions for our members, we offer the best-in-class services for productivity and efficiency.

GROWTH

We keep our members sharp, social and forward thinking by offering educational panels, classes and forums coupled with a variety of cool events.

LEADERSHIP

We offer mentorship programs, educational courses and peer/focus groups to guide members in creating real values for their companies and Africa.

UNITY

We increase visibility for businesses and employment opportunities for locals.  We are shaping our future leaders and giving back to our continent.
